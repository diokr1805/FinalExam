OlsoMet campuses guide!

Campuses

There are two OlsoMet campuses. One is located in the centre of Oslo, and it is the largest among the two. The other campus is located in Kjeller which is near the city of Lillestrøm.

Photo: Oslomet

Pilestredet Campus is the main OsloMet’s campus, and it takes less than 10 min from the central station centre to get there.


Here is the map:Oslo site

Photo: Oslomet

Kjeller Campus is located in Lillestrøm which is only 20 kilometers outside of Oslo and with good connections of public transport.
