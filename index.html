Campuses

OsloMet has two campuses. The largest campus is located in the centre of Oslo, while the other campus is in Kjeller, near the city of Lillestrøm.

Kjeller Campus OsloMet’s second-largest campus lies 20 kilometers outside of Oslo in the fast-growing Romerike region.
