Campus guide

Campuses

OsloMet has two campuses. Our largest campus is located in the centre of Oslo, while our other campus is in Kjeller, near the city of Lillestrøm.

Pilestredet Campus OsloMet’s largest campus is located in the centre of Oslo.

Kjeller Campus OsloMet’s second-largest campus lies 20 kilometers outside of Oslo in the fast-growing Romerike region.
